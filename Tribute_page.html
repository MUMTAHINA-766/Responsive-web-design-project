Yunnan University

The biggest university in Yunnan province

Here's the history of Yunnan University:

  • 1922 - Founded in December
  • 1923 - started to enroll in April
  • 1946 - Yunnan University had five faculties and 18 academic departments, and was included in the Concise Encyclopædia Britannica as one of the fifteen most prestigious universities in China.
